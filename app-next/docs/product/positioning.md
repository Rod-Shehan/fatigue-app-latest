# Product positioning

> **Status:** **Approved 2026-03-18** for app store, marketing, and in-app “about” copy unless superseded by a later revision.

## One-paragraph story

**Fatigue Record** helps commercial drivers and fleet managers record work, rest, and related vehicle context against **Western Australia** workplace health and safety fatigue rules (**OSH (General) Regulations 2022 — Reg 3.132**), with offline-capable sheets, compliance highlights, and manager oversight. The product is being **architected** for **Australia-wide** rule sets in future releases; today’s enforcement logic remains **WA**. This app is **not** an **NHVR-approved Electronic Work Diary (EWD)** unless and until we complete a separate certification and positioning decision — drivers must continue to meet whatever **paper diary, EWD, or jurisdictional** obligations apply to their operations.

## What we are not claiming (until changed)

- **Not** an NHVR **approved EWD** under the Electronic Work Diary Standards.  
- **Not** legal advice; operators should confirm obligations with regulators and their policies.
